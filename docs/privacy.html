Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 21, 2025

At AxelBase, your privacy is not a feature — it’s the entire foundation of this tool.

We Collect Absolutely Nothing

AxelBase is a 100% client-side Base64 encoder and decoder. This means:

  • No servers — no backend, no database, no logs
  • No analytics — no Google Analytics, Plausible, Umami, or any tracking
  • No cookies — not even session cookies
  • No IP logging — we cannot see who you are or where you’re from
  • No file uploads — your files are read locally via the browser’s File API and never leave your device

How It Actually Works

When you:

  • Paste text → processed instantly in your browser memory
  • Drop a file → read using FileReader in chunks, never sent anywhere
  • Click “Copy” or “Download” → handled natively by your browser

All computation happens on your machine. We have zero access.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party scripts, CDNs that track, advertising networks, or social media widgets. The only external resource is Bootstrap CSS (via jsDelivr CDN) for styling — it contains no trackers.

Local Storage & Session Data

The tool does not use localStorage or IndexedDB. Your input is held only in RAM and is cleared when you close or refresh the page.

Security = Privacy by Default

Because nothing ever leaves your browser, AxelBase is safe for:

  • Sensitive documents
  • Private keys & certificates
  • Internal company data
  • Legal or medical files

Children’s Privacy

This tool does not target children and collects no data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (extremely unlikely), the new version will be posted here with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Your data belongs to you — always has, always will.
