Base64 Corruption

7 Most Common Base64 Corruption Patterns (and How to Fix Them)

Published: November 21, 2025

AxelBase detects and warns about these real-world issues instantly:

  1. Missing or incorrect padding (=)
  2. Line breaks or whitespace in the middle
  3. Wrong alphabet (+/ vs -_)
  4. Truncated strings (not mod 4)
  5. Double-encoding
  6. URL percent-encoding applied
  7. Non-Base64 characters present
